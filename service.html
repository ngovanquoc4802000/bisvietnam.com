> SERVICES

In addition to selling testing instruments and consumables, we offer a range of services to support clients in the field of textile testing. Our skilled team of textile testing experts has the necessary experience and knowledge to provide invaluable training and support, and we have a team of service engineers who will undertake service and calibration support.

Any questions, please send us via email info@bisvn.net

SERVICE & CALIBRATION

We provide service and calibration support for test instruments, and we will supply Certificates of Calibration following our visit. Regular maintenance and repairs are critical to ensuring accurate testing, and our service engineers will provide valuable advice while they undertake mechanical maintenance.

TRAINING

We offer training in your laboratory or at our facilities. Our training team has extensive experience from working in the field, and we can provide product training as well as general textile testing support and advice. We can arrange one to one training courses specific to your requirements or we may organize group sessions.

LABORATORY INSTALLATION

We support clients through the complete process of setting up and installing a testing laboratory. We have worked with some companies to establish a new testing laboratory. We will help to define requirements regarding instruments and facilities and then provide consultation for the entire set up process.
